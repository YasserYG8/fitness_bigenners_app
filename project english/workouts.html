Full-Body Workout

  • Jumping Jacks - 30 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats - 15 reps
  • Push-ups (Knee Push-ups for Beginners) - 10 reps
  • Plank - 30 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers - 20 reps
  • Lunges - 10 reps per leg

Upper-Body Workout

  • Push-ups - 12 reps
  • Tricep Dips (use a chair) - 12 reps
  • Plank Shoulder Taps - 20 taps
  • Dumbbell Rows (or water bottles) - 15 reps per arm
  • Arm Circles - 20 reps each direction

Lower-Body Workout

  • Bodyweight Squats - 15 reps
  • Glute Bridges - 20 reps
  • Lunges - 12 reps per leg
  • Wall Sit - 45 seconds
  • Step-Ups (use stairs) - 15 reps per leg
  • Calf Raises - 20 reps

Core Workout

  • Russian Twists - 20 twists
  • Bicycle Crunches - 15 reps per side
  • Leg Raises - 12 reps
  • Mountain Climbers - 20 reps
  • Superman Hold - 30 seconds
